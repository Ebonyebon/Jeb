Q: I have more than one ticket number for medicine collection, can I check the status of these tickets at the same time?
A: You may enter each of your ticket numbers to check the availability of medicines if needed.

Q: How long will the information related to ticket numbers be displayed on “TouchMed”?
A: “TouchMed” only displays information related to ticket numbers issued on the same day. The system will automatically clear the information at 12 midnight every day.

A: Please follow the instructions on the signage or display panel outside the pharmacy, or check with pharmacy staff.
