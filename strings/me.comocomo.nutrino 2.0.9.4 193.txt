Strength training with the proper intensity causes micro-tears in the muscle, which may prompt muscle growth if accompanied by rest and specialized nutrition.

The nutrition required to build muscle efficiently is complex and involves many factors. However, there are two main macronutrients of special importance.

The body requires adequate protein consumption to have sufficient essential amino acids. The essential amino acids are necessary for protein synthesis and muscle growth. It is believed that protein needs to be consumed frequently throughout the day. There is ample results showing that the body absorbs the protein better immediately after anaerobic exercise, and it is typically recommended to eat sufficient protein after a workout and before sleep.
