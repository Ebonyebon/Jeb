    <string name="help_faq">" "<b>1. HOW TO ADD MY IP CAMERA?</b>"

 - Go in \"Manage Cameras\" window. Press Menu and select \"Add camera\". In \"Camera Settings\" you have to specify your camera brand, model, hostname, and port number. Press Back button to see the live video coming out from the camera.

 The easiest way to automatically add all your cameras is to "<b>use Scan Network</b>".



 "<b>2. MY CAMERA IS NOT SUPPORTED. CAN YOU ADD SUPPORT FOR IT?</b>"

 - Please provide us a temp access to your camera (see email in \""<b>About</b>"\" tab). We will take a look.



 "<b>3. I CANNOT ACCESS MY CAMS FROM MOBILE NETWORK, BUT THEY DO WORK WITH LOCAL WIFI CONNECTION. WHAT ARE THE REQUIRED STEPS TO ENABLE BROADCASTING?</b>"

 - You need to setup "<b>port forwarding</b> on your router. Port forwarding forwards all traffic from your private camera to external network, e.g. from <i>192.168.0.3:8080</i> to <i>your_external_ip:8080</i>". Read the router manual for how to do that or contact your system network administrator. Enter your external IP address and port number in tinyCam Monitor when you're done.

 You can also use a free step-by-step tutorial from "<i>www.portforward.com</i>"

 Usually DSL connections renew their IP addresses after 24 hours. "<b>Use DDNS service</b> to get fixed domain name, e.g. <i>www.no-ip.com</i> or <i>www.dyndns.com</i>".



 "<b>4. HOW CAN I CONNECT TO MY USB/LAPTOP CAMERA?</b>"

 - You need to install on your PC either "<b>webcamXP</b>, <b>AbelCam</b> or <b>Yawcam</b>" software which support USB cams broadcasting. For the brand name select Windows in tinyCam Monitor's Camera Settings.

 For Mac computers we support "<b>EvoCam</b>, <b>RemoteSight</b> (recommended), <b>SecuritySpy</b>" software.

 For Linux we support "<b>Motion</b>, <b>MJPG-streamer</b>, <b>ZoneMinder</b>".

 For Android - "<b>IP Webcam</b> (recommended), <b>FRITZ! App Cam Lab</b>, <b>Camera WiFi LiveStream</b>, <b>Ocular IP Webcam.</b>"

 For iOS - "<b>AirBeam</b>, <b>IP Cam Pro</b>, <b>iPCamera - High-End Network Camera</b>" (recommended).



 "<b>5. HOW CAN I VIEW LIVE VIDEO FROM ANOTHER ANDROID DEVICE?</b>"

 - Please install a free Android app called "<b>IP Webcam</b>" on another device. It allows using this device as an IP camera (video, audio, auto focus, LED on/off). Use tinyCam Monitor for IP Webcam monitoring.



 "<b>6. HOW CAN I MONITOR MY WEBCAM?</b>"

 - Open your webcam on a web browser on your PC/Mac. Check live image properties by right clicking on the image and find the full web image address. It should looks like \"http://www.myweb.com/image.jpg\"

 Create a new camera in tinyCam Monitor by using brand \"(Generic)\". Into \"JPEG/MJPEG/RTSP request\" field please enter \"http://www.myweb.com/image.jpg\" (without quotes). You should be able to see live video on your Android device.



 "<b>7. HOW TO ADD MORE WEBCAMS?</b>"

 - To get more webcams please "<b>install free WorldScope Webcams</b> for Android (<i>www.worldscopemobile.com</i>) - official Webcams.travel app for accessing thousands cams database. Start WorldScope app and search for cameras which have <b>Streaming video</b>" capability. tinyCam Monitor will be automatically used for live view, record and import webcams.



 "<b>8. WHAT ABOUT PRIVACY POLICY?</b>"

 - We respect your right to online privacy. tinyCam Monitor does not collect or send us or 3d parties any personal information. The app may collect anonymous usage statistics (can be disabled in App Settings).
